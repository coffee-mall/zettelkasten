There are many different substudies within anatomy. However, two of the main and most common ones are gross anatomy. Gross anatomy is the study of the larger structures that we have within the human body([[1.1 Overview of Anatomy and Physiology - Anatomy and Physiology 2e  OpenStax]]) whereas microscopic anatomy is the study of the different structures only observable with a microscope.
